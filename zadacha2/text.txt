THE LONG DARK is a thoughtful, 
exploration-survival experience that challenges 
solo players to think for themselves as they explore an expansive frozen wilderness in the aftermath of a geomagnetic disaster. There are no zombies -- 
only you, the cold, and all the threats 
Mother Nature can muster.
